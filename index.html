Sushi Yamamoto

鮨やまもと — Asakusa, Tokyo
Sushi / Japanese
🍽 About This Restaurant

A traditional sushi restaurant run by a third-generation sushi chef in the heart of Asakusa. Using fresh fish sourced daily from Toyosu Market, each piece of sushi is carefully crafted with seasonal ingredients. The intimate counter seating allows you to watch the chef prepare your meal right before your eyes.

"We welcome visitors from all over the world. Please enjoy authentic Edomae sushi in a relaxed atmosphere. Don't hesitate to ask us for recommendations!"
